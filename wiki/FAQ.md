# FAQ

## Is Vital client-side?

Yes. Vital is intended as a client-side visual mod.

## Does it require Mod Menu?

No. Mod Menu is optional.

## Can I change the keybinds?

Yes. The keybinds should be editable in Minecraft's standard keybind menu.

## Can I use it in survival mode?

Yes.

## Does it show weapon or armor durability?

No. The current public v1 direction is focused on target health presentation.

## Can I upload it to Modrinth or CurseForge?

Yes, as long as you follow the license and platform policies for your release.
